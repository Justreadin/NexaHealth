NexaHealth Terms of Service

Last updated: 2025

By using NexaHealth, you're joining a trusted community committed to medication safety in Nigeria. These terms help us maintain that trust.

1. Our Health Mission

NexaHealth provides Africa's first AI-powered health trust platform designed to:

  • Verify medication authenticity using NAFDAC records
  • Report suspicious drugs to protect others
  • Provide access to verified drug information
  • Build community-driven medication safety

2. Responsible Use

To maintain trust in our platform:

  • Provide accurate information when verifying medications
  • Only submit legitimate reports of suspicious drugs
  • Never use our system to harass others or spread misinformation
  • Understand we're a supplement to professional medical advice

3. Your Safety Commitments

When you use NexaHealth, you agree:

  • You won't use our verification as sole medical decision-making
  • You'll report suspected fake drugs responsibly
  • You'll keep your account credentials secure
  • You're at least 13 years old (our services aren't for children)

4. Our Promises to You

We commit to:

  • Maintain accurate drug verification systems
  • Process reports of suspicious drugs within 48 hours
  • Protect your personal health data
  • Be transparent about how we operate

5. Data Protection

We handle your information with care:

  • Health reports are anonymized before sharing with NAFDAC
  • We never sell your personal data
  • You can request data deletion anytime

6. Nigerian Law Applies

These terms are governed by Nigerian law. Any disputes will be resolved through Nigerian courts.
