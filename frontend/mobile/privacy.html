NexaHealth Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Encrypted Secure Private

We protect your health data with the same care we'd want for our own.

1. Data We Collect

To provide our health trust services, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email for your profile
  • Health Reports: Drug verifications and reports
  • Technical Data: Device info for security
  • Usage Patterns: How you use our tools

1. You Share Data

When you verify drugs or report issues, we collect necessary details to process your request.

2. We Protect It

Your information is encrypted and stored securely in Nigeria.

3. We Use It Responsibly

Only to provide services and improve medication safety.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us:

  • Verify medication authenticity
  • Identify counterfeit drug patterns
  • Improve our health safety tools
  • Comply with NAFDAC reporting requirements

3. Data Sharing

We only share data when absolutely necessary:

  • With NAFDAC: Anonymized reports of suspicious drugs
  • With You: Your personal health records
  • With Service Providers: Under strict confidentiality

4. Your Rights

You control your data:

  • Access: See what we have about you
  • Correct: Fix any mistakes
  • Delete: Request removal of your data
  • Complain: To Nigerian Data Protection Bureau

5. Our Security Measures

We implement:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Staff privacy training
  • Secure data centers in Nigeria

Our Privacy Promise

We treat your health data with the highest ethical standards, because trust is the foundation of everything we do at NexaHealth.
