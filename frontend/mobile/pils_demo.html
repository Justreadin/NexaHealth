Product Leaflet

Coartem 20/120mg

Novartis • NAFDAC: 04-2345

Tablets

24 Tablets

3 Years

Description

Coartem 20/120mg tablets contain a fixed combination of Artemether and Lumefantrine for the treatment of uncomplicated Plasmodium falciparum malaria in adults and children weighing 5 kg and above.

Indications

  • Treatment of acute uncomplicated malaria caused by Plasmodium falciparum
  • Can be used in areas with chloroquine or multi-drug resistant strains

Pharmacology

Artemether is rapidly metabolized into the active metabolite dihydroartemisinin. Lumefantrine has a long half-life and provides residual antimalarial activity.

Important Safety Information

Do not use in patients with known hypersensitivity to artemether, lumefantrine or any component of the formulation. Not recommended for malaria prophylaxis.
